八字排盤隱私權政策

最後更新：2026 年 7 月 10 日

重點：八字排盤不需要帳號。您輸入的姓名、出生日期、出生時間與命盤資料只儲存在您的裝置上，不會上傳到我們的伺服器。

我們處理的資料

為了排出命盤、合盤、每日運勢、擇日與取名建議，App 會在裝置本機處理您主動輸入的姓名或稱呼、性別、出生日期與出生時間。若您替家人、伴侶或孩子建立資料，請確認您有權在自己的裝置上使用這些資訊。

資料儲存與刪除

  • 命主資料與付費解鎖狀態使用 Apple 提供的本機資料儲存技術保存在裝置上。
  • App 不提供帳號或雲端同步，也不把命盤資料傳送給外部 AI 服務。
  • 您可以在 App 內刪除個別命主資料。移除 App 也會移除 App 的本機資料。

App 內購買

命格詳批與取名服務的付款由 Apple App Store 與 StoreKit 處理。我們不會取得您的信用卡號或完整付款資料。Apple 可能依其隱私權政策處理交易、裝置與帳號資訊。

通知、分析與追蹤

若您主動允許通知，App 只會依您選擇的功能安排裝置通知。App 不含第三方廣告 SDK、不進行跨 App 追蹤，也不出售個人資料。

資料安全與兒童資料

資料留在您的裝置上，安全性也取決於裝置密碼、Apple 帳號與作業系統安全設定。App 可能被成人用來輸入孩子的取名資料，但不會要求孩子建立帳號，也不會刻意從孩子蒐集資料。

政策更新與聯絡方式

功能或法律要求改變時，我們可能更新本政策。隱私問題可透過支援頁面提出。

Privacy Policy for Bazi Chart

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Summary: Bazi Chart does not require an account. Names, birth dates, birth times, and chart data you enter are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Data the App Processes

The App processes names or labels, gender, birth dates, and birth times that you voluntarily enter to calculate charts, compatibility, daily readings, date selection, and naming suggestions. If you enter information about a family member, partner, or child, make sure you are authorized to use it on your device.

Storage and Deletion

  • Profiles and purchase-unlock status are stored locally using Apple-provided storage technology.
  • The App has no account or cloud-sync feature and does not send chart data to an external AI service.
  • You can delete individual profiles in the App. Removing the App also removes its local data.

In-App Purchases

Payments for detailed readings and naming services are processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive your credit-card number or complete payment details.

Notifications, Analytics, and Tracking

If you enable notifications, the App schedules device notifications only for features you choose. The App contains no third-party advertising SDK, does not track you across apps, and does not sell personal data.

Security, Children, and Contact

Local data security depends on your device passcode, Apple Account, and operating-system security. Adults may enter a child's information for naming purposes, but the App does not ask children to create an account or intentionally collect data directly from children. Questions can be submitted through the support page.
